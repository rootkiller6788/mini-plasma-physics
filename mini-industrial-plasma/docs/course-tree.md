# Course Dependency Tree - mini-industrial-plasma
## Prerequisites
- mini-classical-mechanics: Newtonian mechanics, energy conservation
- mini-electromagnetism: Maxwell equations, Poisson equation
- mini-thermodynamics-statphys: Boltzmann distribution, kinetic theory
- mini-quantum-mechanics: Cross sections, collision theory (L6 level)
- mini-fluid-dynamics: Fluid equations, transport coefficients

## Core (this module)
- Plasma state and parameters
- Sheath physics (Bohm, Child-Langmuir)
- Discharge physics (DC, RF, CCP, ICP)
- Electron kinetics (EEDF, Boltzmann)
- Plasma chemistry (Ar, SF6, SiH4, O2)
- Surface interactions (etching, deposition, sputtering)

## Dependent Modules
- mini-nuclear-physics: Fusion plasma boundary, wall interactions
- mini-particle-physics: Plasma-based particle sources
- mini-astrophysics: Space plasma, solar wind
- mini-computational-physics: PIC/MCC plasma simulation methods
- mini-interdisciplinary-physics: Plasma medicine, plasma agriculture
